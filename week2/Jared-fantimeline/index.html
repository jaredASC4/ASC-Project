Travis Scott

is one of the greatest rappers alive as of July 24th, 2017. Travis Scott has produced some of the most played songs of 2015-2017 and is taking the Rap Game by storm.

1992: Travis Scott Was Born

Although Travis Scott is his stage name, Jacques Webster was born on April 30th, 1992 in Houston, Texas. Travis always manages to pay homage to his hometown by giving a special performance when he visits on tour.

2008 -2012: Travis' Early Career Beginnings

While living in Houston,Texas, Travis managed to form a music group with his classmates and produced early music. Years later, Travis got into contact with Atlanta rapper T.I and gained attention through him.

2012 and 2014: Owl Pharoah and Days Before Rodeo

In 2012, Travis released his first full-length mixtape called Owl Pharoah which was also re-styled by Kanye West and Mike Dean. Later on, Kanye signed Scott onto his label G.O.O.D Music. Two years later Scott dropped Days Before Rodeo which featured many huge and up and coming artists.
